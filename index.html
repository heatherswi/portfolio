about me



As a freelance Illustrator and UX/UI designer, and current student at UTSA studying Film & Media, I’m passionate about visual design and creating enjoyable user experiences.

I enjoy elegant and cute designs, opting for pastel colors and patterns over simple designs. I would love to design a website for women’s fashion and makeup, or one focused on cute things like pets, kids, or games!

I also enjoy playing video games and admiring (or critiquing) the UI elements and user experience! I would also love to work on a game’s menus and HUD, making it as visually appealing and user friendly as possible.
